About Hospital

Established in 2002, Life Care hospital started as a single specialty clinic in a small town area of Chauri Chaura, Uttar Pradesh. Today, Life Care Hospital is a Multi-specialty 100 bedded Hospital in Gorakhpur. Life Care Hospital is a multi-specialty family-run hospital located in the heart of the metropolitan city of Gorakhpur. Committed to supreme patient care, all family members are specialist doctors in different fields of surgery and medicine forming a strong web of multiple specialties under one roof. Focus on providing cross reference consultancy to almost all patients, results in minimum error cases. With integrity at the core of its service, Life Care believes in supreme patient well-being and satisfaction at all times. Driven by Passion to serve, we focus on constant improvement to provide quality healthcare services to all patients. We are associated with the Ayushman Bharat Yojana (PMJAY) scheme, aiming to provide free quality healthcare to the under privileged. We continue to align ourselves with Government Schemes in lieu of our commitment to provide quality healthcare to all. With trust and devotion, we hope to associate with you in your well-being.
